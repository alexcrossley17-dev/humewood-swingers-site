Who We Are

About the Society

The Humewood Swingers are a social golf society based in Hong Kong, playing out of Kau Sai Chau Public Golf Course. Founded by a group of mates who love golf, good times, and the occasional braai, we've formalised things for the 2026/27 season with teams, competitions, and plenty of fines.

How We Play

Competitions

Season-long glory and on-the-day bragging rights

Season-Long (Continuous)

01
Race to Shek O Beach

Rolling Team Net. Rolling net scores after each round. The amount of net scores to count will be determined by the minimum participation across the field on the day.

02
Single Golden Cap

Annual Individual Stableford. Rolling stableford points. Best 5 to count over the season.

On The Day

03
Jackets

On the Day Jacket Pairings. Randomly paired with a partner irrespective of teams. Best combined stableford points. Winning pairs defend title at next round.

04
2 Club & Jackpot

On the day prize. 20 points to enter: 50/50 split. 2-Club: Qualify for a share of the pot by getting a 2 on your scorecard. Jackpot: Highest accumulated stableford points by an individual on selected holes takes home the pot.

05
Closest to the Pin

Nearest the flag on selected par 3s. Bragging rights and a cold one on the house.

06
Longest Drive

Bomb it off the tee on a selected hole. Fairway only counts — the rough is for amateurs.
